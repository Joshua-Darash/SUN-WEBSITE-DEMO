RESPONSIBILITIES HELD BY THE ABOVE OFFICES
Vice Chancellor:
The office of the Vice Chancellor is responsible for every activities that takes place within the university campus, and also governs all the other offices in the
university.

Academic Registror:
The office of the Academic Registrar is responsible for every academic activities that takes place in the university, including examinations and issuing of results.

Dean of students:
The Office of the Dean of Students is responsible for the students welfare.
